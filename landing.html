What makes it different

Designed for
discovery, not lists.

Most quote apps give you one quote at a time. Verbatim gives you a living wall — every visit is different, every scroll reveals something unexpected.

01
Infinite Mosaic
Quotes are arranged in a typographic grid that stretches in every direction. Drag, scroll, and pan like a map.
02
Physics Scroll
Drag and release — the canvas coasts with natural inertia. Works with mouse, trackpad, touch, and keyboard arrows.
03
Fade-in Reveal
Every quote fades in as it enters view. The wall comes alive as you explore it — no two sessions look the same.
04
Works Offline
Install once, access forever. A service worker caches everything so Verbatim works without a connection.
05
Editorial Typography
Three typefaces — Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, and Fragment Mono — matched to the character of each quote.
06
Installs Like an App
Progressive Web App. Add to your home screen on Android or iOS — no app store required. Fullscreen, no browser chrome.

Install guide

On your phone
in 30 seconds.

No app store. No download. Just add to your home screen and it works like a native app — offline included.

Android · Chrome

  1. Open verbatim.app in Chrome
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top right
  3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add on the confirmation dialog
  5. Verbatim appears on your home screen — tap to launch fullscreen

iPhone / iPad · Safari

  1. Open verbatim.app in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (the box with an arrow) at the bottom
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add in the top right corner
  5. Verbatim appears on your home screen — no browser chrome, full screen
